Ingredients for pork ramen

  • tonkotsu pork broth
  • ramen noodles

  • green onions
  • pork chasu

Steps to make pork ramen

  1. Make pork chasu
  2. Make pork broth
  3. Combine pork chasu, pork broth, and vegetables
